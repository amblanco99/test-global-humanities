Core courses

The core courses for the Global Humanities Certificate are concerned with borderlands, zones of interaction between cultures where we can observe mutual influence, imitation, and change, as well as processes of erasure and destruction. The disciplines of the humanities (literature, philosophy, history, the study of art and culture) allow us to examine the human record of this interaction (artistic, documentary, cultural) and the forms of expression it creates or makes impossible. Focusing on a comparative exploration of distinctive contexts and their complexity, as well as on the collaborative work between the course participants, the course uses the approaches of the fields of the humanities to consider what has been produced by cultural interrelationships across time and space, and reflect on its meaning and significance for the problems of the contemporary world.
